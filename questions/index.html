  1. Are there collectible weapons, or do you stay with a certain weapon for the majority of the game?

    Weapons are collectable and you can switch weapons, but you can only hold one weapon at a time.

  2. Can you upgrade your weapon? Is the weapon system linear or is it more of a tech tree?

    You can't upgrade weapons.

  3. Will weapons have different playstyles?

    Every weapon gives a special perk when equipped.
